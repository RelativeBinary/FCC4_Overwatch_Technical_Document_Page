TL;DR: Here you can find tactics and conceptual countering tips you can use, rather than screaming at your team. This information is primarily from Your Overwatch and Level 5 Pidgey, i've made this to act as a compilation for such information.

Welcome to this guide on playing Overwatch, one of the more unique FPS games which has come to be a favourite of mine. This guide will cover general key tactics and conceptual countering tips you can use in your role, to really get the most out of your own responsibilities in your team. I'll also try to add in some more detailed information for each hero, although there are already a lot of heros in the game.

What you'll find in this guide:
  • What you really need to know in a quick list (like this).
  • Information about your role and/or specifically a heros role
  • Tactical ideas for your role and/or specifically a heros role
  • Tips in playing certain heros or what you could do in certain situations
    •
    Most of the bigger chunks of text's will be examples, which i think are better to represent the reasoning for when to use certain tactics. All information will be comming from Your Overwatch's and Level 5 Pidgey extrememly hard work in supporting the community. This is in no means a replacement but rather a fast way to get some information. I really recommend you check out their channel to get super in depth and insightful information.

    Overall, I hope this guide will be of use for many Overwatch players who are looking to improve their game skill little by little.
